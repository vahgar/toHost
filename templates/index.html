About Us

Ishan Creations was created & founded by Rajneesh Kumar Malhotra in 2015. At Ishan Creations, Our aim from inception is to put the needs of others first, and our commitment to give back Value for money to them. Ishan Creations' Motto is “Get More. SMARTLY”

Ishan Creations is the World’s 1st and Unique innovative concept with a:

• Vision - वसुधैव कुटुम्बकम (Vasudhaiv Kutumbakam)-The Whole World is a Family
• Mission - Empowering Consumer and Strengthening Business

It gives a platform to those Entrepreneurs / Business entities that are committed to provide Best Quality Products & Services to Our Consumer members at a reasonable competitive Price and eager to promote this awareness to everyone.

For Consumer, Our objective is to make home consumables conveniently available with Value for money. We at Ishan Creations aim to provide Benefits in your Daily needs to Shopping, Dining to Entertainment, Fitness to Wellness and many more services with Trust.

For Business entity, We help to get Better ROI by strengthening their business with a long term Success and Growth.

Our members are our family and we want to build Trust and Confidence in Consumer and Business entities to serve right & better by understanding their preferences and have a genuine, mutual and profound long term relationship with lots of Privileges & Benefits.
